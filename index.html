Street Sidekick

We are strong, independent women. We should be able to feel safe on a morning run by ourselves or a solo adventure in a new city. We shouldn't have to face barriers, like street harassment, to traveling or walking home alone.

Street Sidekick is here to empower and keep you safe, so you don't have to miss all the fun. From outside the bar to your friends' downtown loft, to the pizza place around the corner, and to your doorstep, we are with you all the way.
